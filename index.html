Welcome to Bridge Care

Bridge Care is a mobile health service aimed at connecting rural and underserved communities with consistent, affordable, and compassionate healthcare — both physical and mental. We believe no one should be left behind when it comes to getting care.

Our Services

  • Mobile Physical Health Check-ups
  • Mental Health Counseling and Support
  • Telehealth Appointments with Specialists
  • Preventative Screenings
  • Prescription Delivery in Partnership with Local Clinics
  • Extended After-Work Hours Services

About Bridge Care

Bridge Care was created to fix the growing gap in healthcare access for rural and low-income Americans. We’re inspired by existing groups like Remote Area Medical, but we go further by offering long-term mobile clinics that consistently return to the same communities.

Our model centers around trust, cultural understanding, and building real relationships with the people we serve.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?
Appointments are available on-site or can be scheduled through our mobile app and hotline.

Do you offer mental health services?
Yes. Bridge Care combines mental and physical healthcare in one mobile unit to make it easier for patients to get complete care.

Where do you operate?
We serve rural and medically underserved counties across the United States and return regularly to the same locations.
