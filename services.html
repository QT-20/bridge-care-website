Welcome to Bridge Care

Mission Statement: Bridge Care is dedicated to bridging the healthcare access gap by providing long-term, mobile medical and mental health services to rural and underserved communities across America. Our mission is to ensure that no individual is left behind when it comes to receiving essential healthcare.

Millions of Americans are still without proper access to healthcare, especially those living in rural or economically disadvantaged areas. Bridge Care tackles this issue by offering mobile clinics staffed with medical professionals that deliver ongoing physical and mental health support. Unlike temporary pop-up clinics, Bridge Care returns regularly, builds community trust, and integrates telehealth services to broaden access. We partner with local hospitals, organizations, and donors to create lasting, affordable solutions.

Services Offered

  • Mobile Medical Clinics with Regular Scheduling
  • Mental Health Counseling & Support Services
  • Telehealth Access to Out-of-State Specialists
  • Prescription Coordination with Local Pharmacies
  • Preventative Care & Chronic Illness Screenings
  • Health Education and Awareness Campaigns
  • After-hours Care Availability

About Bridge Care

Bridge Care was born out of the need to provide affordable, consistent healthcare access for low-income families, rural citizens, and people living with disabilities. Inspired by gaps in existing organizations like Remote Area Medical and The Health Wagon, our organization combines physical and mental healthcare into one traveling service. With consistent community visits, culturally competent care, and local partnerships, Bridge Care becomes a reliable part of every community it serves.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I sign up for services? Visit our Contact page or connect with us at a local pop-up event or online form.
  • Are your services free? Many services are free or offered at extremely low cost thanks to grants and donations.
  • How often do you return to locations? Every 4-6 weeks, based on need and scheduling.
  • Do you offer pediatric services? Yes! We provide care for all age groups including children and teens.
  • Can I get mental health support here? Absolutely. We have trained professionals for therapy and psychiatric care.
